About Us

What is PBL15?

PBL15 is a Problem-Based Learning project by medical students at Horus University. Our mission is to raise awareness about breast cancer, particularly focusing on the relationship between hormonal contraception and cancer risk in BRCA mutation carriers.

Through research, educational materials, and community outreach, we aim to empower women with knowledge to make informed health decisions.
